Welcome

Join our sustainable community network for seed swapping, growing education, and food exchange. Introduction

Climate change threatens us all. We must also ensure that healthy food options are accessible and affordable. SeedCities wants to transform our ecosystem by connecting the community.
Watch Intro

Community

Connect with farmers, gardeners, and urban growers to plan what you want to grow, when to grow it, and how to transform your space for urban agriculture. Find and join a community garden and work with others, or start your own community garden.

Guide

There are numerous strategies that can be implemented and our guide will help you decide which will work best for you. Different plants have different needs when it comes to space and treatment, whether indoors, outdoors, a balcony, or a rooftop. We have the resources for you.

Exchange

Exchange resources, information, tips and tricks with everyone from expert to beginner. Participate in a "Seed Swap", co-operative harvest and exchange, farmer's market, or tools with others to make an impact in your community.
